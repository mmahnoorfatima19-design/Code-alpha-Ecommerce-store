Our Story

Paucek & Lage Alpha ki shuruat ek chhote vision ke saath hui thi: logon ko quality products ghar baithe provide karna. Humne dekha ki market mein reliable cheezein milna mushkil hai, isliye humne khud ek platform banaya jahan quality aur trust ka sabse zyada khayal rakha jata hai.

Journey started from

Humne 2020 mein apni journey start ki. Ek chhote se room se lekar aaj ek online platform tak, hamara safar mehnat aur customer feedback par based hai. Har saal humne apne products aur delivery system ko behtar banaya taake aap tak sirf behtareen cheez pohanche.

Our Growth (2020 - Present)

2020 mein hamara focus sirf local products par tha, lekin ab hum apni range ko expand kar chuke hain. Humne technology aur security mein invest kiya hai taake aapki har transaction safe aur fast ho.
